Why This System Matters

Identifying suspects from witness voice descriptions can be challenging and time-consuming. Our AI-powered system converts spoken descriptions into realistic suspect faces, compares them with an investigation database, and identifies the most similar suspect to assist investigators while ensuring that final identification remains a human decision.

Forensic Investigation Workflow

1
Witness Description

The investigation begins when a witness provides a detailed description of the suspect's facial characteristics.

2
AI Face Generation

Artificial Intelligence converts the witness description into a realistic suspect face for further analysis.

3
Face Recognition & Feature Extraction

The generated face is analyzed, and unique facial features are extracted using deep learning algorithms.

4
Database Comparison

The extracted facial features are compared against the investigation database to identify potential matches.

5
Best Match Identification

The system compares the generated suspect face with the investigation database and identifies the single most similar individual along with the similarity percentage for investigator review.

6
Investigation Report

A standardized investigation report is generated, summarizing the generated suspect image, the identified matching individual, the similarity score, and recommendations for further investigation.
